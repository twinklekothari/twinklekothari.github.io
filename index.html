About Me

I am Twinkle Kothari, a Ph.D. student in Aerospace Engineering at the University of Texas at Arlington, affiliated with the AIM³ Lab under the guidance of Dr. Xin Liu, and working with UTARI – IPPM.

I enjoy solving complex structural challenges through a combination of simulation, validation, and collaborative research.
